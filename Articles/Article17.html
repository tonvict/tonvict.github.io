Top 5 Things Only Haley Joel Osment Can See

March 2, 2018

  1. The Angel And Devil suggesting what you should do when in a moral quandry, he can see all of yours but not his own.
  2. A little gnome that likes to steal little kids eyelashes; at first he thought it was super creepy, but over time has come to appreciate all that the gnome is doing for society.

  5. Bruce Willis's ghost, which is especially scary because Bruce Willis isn't even dead yet.
