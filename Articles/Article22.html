Frat Bro Drowns While Snorkeling

March 2, 2018

MEXICO- In a shocking tragedy that no one saw coming, fraternity member Alex “A-Dog” Ramirez drowned while snorkeling on a Pi Kappa Epsilon trip to Cabos San Lucas. Witnesses explained to authorities that they heard his fellow fraternity members, start chanting “Chug! Chug! Chug!” and they saw Mr. Ramirez dunk himself and his snorkel into the ocean and balloon to 5 times his original size."

"Apparently Mr. Ramirez drowned in an attempt to chug down the entire Pacific Ocean." Explained authorities.
