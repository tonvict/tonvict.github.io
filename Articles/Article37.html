Local Man says "We Get it, You're Gay" To God Every Time He Sees a Rainbow

March 23, 2018

A visibly annoyed Kurt Tillerson couldn’t help but mutter angrily to himself after discovering a rainbow forming outside his kitchen window. Kurt explained to reporters that he doesn’t mind that God is a homosexual, he just “wishes that he didn’t have to rub it in everyone's face all the time.”

Local meteorologist James Mandelbaum explained to Tillerson that the rainbow was not an attempt for God to flaunt his homosexuality but rather a natural phenomenon that shows pride in something that was often seen as a negative quality in the past.
