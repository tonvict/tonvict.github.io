Swastikas for Potstickas

April 8, 2018



SEATTLE- An exciting new charity has sprouted in the heart of Seattle. In an effort to curb interest for white supremacist groups, Toshi’s Teriyaki, a restaurant in the cities international district, is providing the community with the “Swastikas for Potstickas” campaign.

The campaign urges reformed Nazi’s to trade in their Nazi paraphernalia for delicious fried dumplings. Restaurant owner Toshi Yamamoto stated that he “hopes people's love for fried dumplings outways people's hate for Jews.” With potstickers this good, it’s gonna be hard to turn down!
